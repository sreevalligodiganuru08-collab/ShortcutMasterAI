The mission behind ShortcutMaster AI and how we help users reach elite computer productivity.

The Core Problem

Every year, developers and business professionals waste hundreds of collective hours using the computer mouse for tasks that could easily be performed in milliseconds with keyboard shortcut combinations. The barrier to mastery has always been dry, plain documentation tables and a lack of active muscle memory practice.

Our Gamified SaaS Solution

ShortcutMaster AI provides an interactive, hands-on visual canvas that records key combinations in real time. By mapping keys onto a virtual floating glass keyboard and feeding accuracy ratings into our background AI Recommendation model, we deliver bite-sized training sessions specifically for your weak points.

System Specifications

We support complete layouts for VS Code, Figma, Google Chrome, Excel, Git, macOS terminal, and general system actions. With automatic support for both standard Windows and Cmd-based Apple layouts, ShortcutMaster AI adapts to whatever workstation you use.
